UK Net Zero Carbon Buildings Standard

The UK Net Zero Carbon Buildings Standard will enable industry to robustly prove their built assets are net zero carbon, joined by industry organisations BBP, BRE, the Carbon Trust, CIBSE, IStructE, LETI, RIBA, RICS, and UKGBC. This is the method underlying the new SAP / RdSAP 11 Standard Assessment Procedure for EPC

Standard Assessment Procedure

Guidance on how a home's energy performance is calculated using the Standard Assessment Procedure (SAP) methodology, which underpins the Energy Performance Certificate (EPC). The new SAP / RdSAP 11 (scoped by Etude and brought forward by BRE) will come into force in 2025, alongside the Future Homes Standard update of the Building Regulations.

CalTrack

CalTRACK specifies a set of empirically tested methods to standardize the way normalized meter-based changes in energy consumption are measured and reported. When CalTRACK is implemented through open source software, these methods can be used to support procurement of energy efficiency, electrification, and other distributed energy resources.
