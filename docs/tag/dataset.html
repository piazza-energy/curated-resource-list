Nord Pool

Nord Pool runs the leading power market in Europe, and we offer day-ahead and intraday markets to our customers. The day-ahead market is the main arena for trading power, and the intraday market supplements the day-ahead market and helps secure balance between supply and demand. Historical datasets are available on their website.
