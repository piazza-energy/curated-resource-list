Energy Web (EW) is a global nonprofit organization accelerating a low-carbon, customer-centric electricity system. EW focuses on building core infrastructure and shared technology. After launching their enterprise blockchain platform tailored to the energy sector in 2019, EW’s technology roadmap has since grown into the Energy Web Decentralized Operating System (EW-DOS), a full stack that includes front-end applications and a variety of software development toolkits.
